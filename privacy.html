Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

For Parents: vote.rizz is designed for kids! We take privacy seriously and follow COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) rules. This page explains exactly what information we collect and how we protect your child.

What is vote.rizz?

vote.rizz is a fun app where kids can share pictures of cool stuff (NOT faces or personal photos!) and rate how "fire" things are. Think of it like rating your favorite toys, drawings, or food pictures!

What Information Do We Collect?

  • Username: A fun nickname (not your real name)
  • Password: To keep your account safe
  • Parent Email: So we can get permission from your parent/guardian
  • Birthday: To make sure we follow the rules for kids
  • Content You Post: Pictures and videos you choose to share (no faces allowed!)
  • Votes and Comments: Your ratings and what you say about posts

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real name
  • Your address
  • Your school name
  • Your phone number
  • Photos of your face or body
  • Location data

How Do We Use Your Information?

  • To let you log in and use the app
  • To show your posts to other users
  • To keep the app safe and block rule-breakers
  • To contact parents if there's a safety concern

Parent/Guardian Consent

Important: If you are under 13 years old, we MUST get permission from your parent or guardian before you can use vote.rizz.

When you sign up, we'll ask for a parent's email address. Your parent will receive an email and must click a link to approve your account. You cannot use the app until they approve.

How Do We Keep Information Safe?

  • Passwords are protected (hashed) so nobody can see them
  • We use secure connections (HTTPS)
  • Our team reviews content to remove anything inappropriate
  • We have automatic filters to block bad words
  • Face detection blocks photos with faces

Who Can See Your Information?

  • Your username and posts: Other users can see these
  • Your password: NOBODY can see this, not even us!
  • Parent email: Only our admin team (to contact about safety issues)

Parent Rights

Parents can:

  • Review their child's account information
  • Request deletion of their child's account and all data
  • Refuse to allow further collection of their child's information
  • Contact us at any time with questions

How Long Do We Keep Data?

We keep account data as long as the account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your data within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes, we'll update this page and notify parents via email if the changes affect how we handle children's information.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your parental rights, contact us:

Email: privacy@voterizz.com

We respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
