Welcome to vote.rizz!

These rules help keep everyone safe and make sure vote.rizz is fun for everyone. By using vote.rizz, you agree to follow these rules!

Who Can Use vote.rizz?

  • vote.rizz is for kids and teens
  • If you're under 13, you need permission from a parent or guardian
  • Your parent must verify their email before you can use the app

Safety Rules - SUPER IMPORTANT!

  • NO pictures showing your face - This is for your safety!
  • NO pictures showing your body - Keep yourself private!
  • NO sharing personal info - No phone numbers, addresses, school names, or last names
  • NO asking others for personal info - Don't be creepy!
  • Keep your password secret - Don't share it with anyone, not even friends!

Content Rules - What You CAN'T Post

  • No mean comments or bullying
  • No making fun of people
  • No swear words or bad language
  • No slurs of any kind
  • No inappropriate pictures or videos
  • No violent content
  • No flirting or "dating" posts
  • No calling people "hot" or commenting on looks
  • No pretending to be someone else
  • No spam or posting the same thing over and over

What CAN You Post?

vote.rizz is for sharing cool stuff! Good posts include:

  • Pictures of your drawings or art
  • Photos of cool objects, toys, or collections
  • Food pictures
  • Nature photos (no people!)
  • Pet pictures
  • Memes (appropriate ones!)
  • Anything cool that doesn't show people!

How Voting Works

You can rate posts from 1-10 based on how cool/fire they are. Be nice with your ratings - remember there's a real person who posted it!

What Happens If You Break Rules

  • First time: Warning
  • Second time: Your post gets deleted
  • Third time: Your account gets banned
  • Serious violations: Immediate ban (no warnings for really bad stuff)

Reporting

If you see something that breaks the rules or makes you uncomfortable, use the Report button! Our team will review it quickly. You can also tell a parent or trusted adult.

Your Account

  • You're responsible for everything that happens on your account
  • Don't share your password
  • If someone else is using your account, tell us immediately
  • We can suspend or delete accounts that break rules

Changes to These Terms

We might update these rules sometimes. If we make big changes, we'll let you know when you log in.

Questions?
